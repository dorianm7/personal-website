Extracurriculars

KSDT Radio DJ

Driven to expose the deeper lesser known side of the Dubstep and bass heavy genres in electronic music, every week I would host my radio show Dubstep & Bass Shoe at KSDT in hopes of educating the masses. KSDT is UC San Diego's only student run radio station. From Dub to Dubstep, Dancehall, Garage, and Grime going all the way to Trap, Soundcloud Rap, and Future Beats you always knew you would hear something new.

Triton's For Sally Ride Science Member

What drove me towards the Triton's For Sally Ride Science organization was they're inclusion of the Arts as a part of STEAM education. Having a love for the arts and young nieces that love painting and drawing, I find the arts extremely important in helping express one's own creativity feelings in a positive manner. TFSRS has helped me share my passion of helping others and inspiring the next generation of Austronaut Ballerina Artists.
