Projects

Rüm8

Android | Java | Firebase

Rüm8, an Android application made for students looking to find a roomate that is compatible with their lifestyle.

As a User Interface Specialist I was tasked in helping the user have a positive interaction with our application. Through this role I learned how crucial research is in the development of a product. Through thorough research into the best practices and components available I was able to create designs that created a roadmap for the software leads to implement.

Clubhouse Dungeons

Chrome | Javascript | Puppeteer

Clubhouse Dungeons, the solution to agile teams productivity while working from home. Clubhouse Dungeons is a Google Chrome Extension that is used as an extension of Clubhouse, the project management software. It gamifies your sprints and user stories into a competition to attempt to reach the leaderboard and defeat the boss. Leading a team of 13 helped show me how important communication and retrospection is for the success of the team. We need to be able to learn to communicate the failures and successes of the past to be better prepared for the future.
